The "Broken" Correlation

Our regression analysis shows a coefficient of -0.009 between review scores and playtime. High-scoring narrative games often experience immediate churn after completion.

Strategic Pivot

Invest in "Systemic Replayability". Mechanics that offer 100+ hours (Simulation, Strategy) generate 4x the long-term ROI compared to cinematic titles.

Top Yield Genres

Estimated revenue is highest in Action Roguelikes and Simulation. These tags correlate with higher price retention and lower discount elasticity.

Market Tip

"Female Protagonist" is no longer a niche tag—it now represents a core revenue driver with high advocacy ratios.

Tier 1 Dominance

USA, UK, and China account for 65% of all helpful reviews. These regions drive the Steam discovery algorithm.

Emerging Markets

Brazil and Turkey show high review-per-user volume but lower revenue density, suggesting a need for localized Pricing (PPP).

Lore-Rich Systems

Prioritize development of IPs that fuel "Theory-Crafting". High community engagement on wikis and YouTube acts as zero-cost marketing during sales.
